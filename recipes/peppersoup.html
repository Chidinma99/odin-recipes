Peppersoup Recipe

Description

This pepper soup is a delicious Nigerian recipe that combines the aromatic flavours of ehuru with traditional goat’s meat. Don’t forget to add a generous amount of ground crayfish for extra flavour! Simply follow our easy step-by-step recipe and you’ll soon be enjoying a hearty portion of proudly Nigerian food.

Ingredients

  • 10 iwo/ehuru
  • 2 urheri/uda
  • 2 umilo/ulima
  • 1/2 tsb ataiko/atariko
  • 21/2 tsb irugeje
  • Scent leaves (optional)
  • tbsp. ground crayfish
  • seasoning cubes
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • tbsp. ground pepper
  • 1 kg goat’s meat

Steps

  1. Cut goat’s meat into bite-size pieces, wash and place in a pot with water, season with salt, onions and seasoning cubes. Allow to boil for 20 min. Add more water.
  2. Crack open the umilo, gbafilo and iwo, blend the contents alongside ataiko and irugeje. Add all ground spices – pepper, ataiko, irugeje, iwo, umilo, gbafilo, crayfish and deseeded urheri into the pot as well. Alternatively, you can purchase pre-blended pepper soup spice and add 2 tablespoons.
  3. Add some chopped fresh pepper.
  4. Cover the pot and allow to cook – the pepper soup should be ready in 35 min.
  5. Add scent leaves and allow to simmer for 5 min.
